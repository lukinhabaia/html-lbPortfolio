The Best Movies According to Lucca

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

1. Interstellar

It's got a great soundtrack and the visuals are absolutely insane, there's no words to describe the movie itself...awesome sci-fi with a great plot and characters.

2. Scarface

Another great movie with an awesome plot about crime and drug trafficking with really good characters and story.

3. Inception

Like Interstellar, was also directed by Nolan and involves a confusing and intriguing plot. A really great movie which keeps you at the edge of your seat.
